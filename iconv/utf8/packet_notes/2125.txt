                <p style="font-family: courier">
                        <b>Notes: This is a merger proposal between Szabo's 02333 MFA CB1+, Szabo's 02332 Basket CB1+, Szabo's 02232 RSBC, and Biro's new 02332 MFA CCK+. The axons in CA3:SO for the 02333 cell are very sparse and are now considered to be sub-threshold for the sake of this merger proposal. The axons in CA3:SL for the 02232 cell are no longer considered to be sub-threshold for the sake of this merger proposal. These proposed merger cells do not have dendrites in CA3:SLM, which distinguishes them from the two v1.0 active neuron types. These proposed merger cells have axons in CA3:SP, which distinguishes them from the 02322 cells. These proposed merger cells have an above threshold presence of dendrites in CA3:SO, which distinguishes them from the 02230 cells. These proposed merger cells are either CB1+, CCK+, or regular spiking, which will distinguish them from PV+ fast-spiking cells when they are discovered in the literature.</b>
